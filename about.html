About BDWU

The Botswana Diamond Workers Union (BDWU) represents workers across the diamond and hospitality sectors. We are dedicated to improving pay, protecting benefits, and empowering workers through organizing and collective bargaining.

Our Vision

A fair, safe and dignified workplace for all employees in Botswana's diamond and hospitality industries.
